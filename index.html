About Me

Well if I had to tell you something about me, there is this memory that comes to my mind. When I had just gone to the 12th standard we got quarantined due to covid. In the previous year I had chosen Computer Science as I was very fascinated by computers. Those little tinker boxes which always did as told and I wanted to know more about them. Then when the covid came along it gave me an opportunity to observe just how much the world could achieve using computers and Programming. And during this period of online classes was when I first used programming to solve a problem related to me. It was a simple script that helped me join the classes on time. It worked well in my case, it even inspired me to use programming to solve problems that I faced in my day-to-day life. This was the moment when I knew that I wanted to be a software engineer. I was not told to be one.
